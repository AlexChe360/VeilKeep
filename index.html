VeilKeep — Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 6, 2025

At a glance

  • Sign‑in uses the third‑party service Supabase (authentication only).
  • All note content, checklists, and attachments are stored locally on your device and encrypted. The encryption key is kept locally; we do not have access to it.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or third‑party analytics SDKs.

1) Who we are

VeilKeep (the “App”, “we”, “us”, “our”). Developer contact is in the Contact us section.


2) Data we process

2.1 Authentication (via Supabase)

  • Email address and/or other identifier used to sign in.
  • Authentication tokens required to maintain your session.

These items are processed by Supabase solely to provide the sign‑in function. We do not store your password.

2.2 On‑device data (local)

  • Notes and attachments (e.g., photos, documents, scans).
  • Encryption keys and metadata needed for offline storage.

This data does not leave your device unless you choose to export/share it.

2.3 Diagnostics and logs

By default, we do not collect analytics. Optional OS/app‑store crash reports may be sent if you enabled them on your device and can include anonymous technical information (device model, OS version, etc.).


3) Purposes & legal bases

  • Contract performance: provide sign‑in and maintain sessions (via Supabase).
  • Legitimate interests: security, abuse prevention, stability (limited diagnostics).
  • Consent: any optional device permissions/features you explicitly enable.

4) Storage & retention

  • Auth tokens may be stored in secure device storage (e.g., Keychain/Keystore) and are removed on sign‑out or app deletion.
  • Notes data remains locally for as long as you keep it in the App.
  • Supabase account data (e.g., your email) is retained until you delete the account.

5) Where processing occurs

Authentication is handled by Supabase on its infrastructure. The project region is defined by our Supabase configuration and may be outside your country. The active region will be disclosed in the App and/or on our website.


6) Sharing with third parties

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Supabase acts as our processor for sign‑in only and processes data on our behalf.
  • Data may be disclosed if required by law (e.g., a valid court order) and only to the legally required extent.

7) Security

  • Your notes are encrypted locally; the key stays on your device and is never sent to us.
  • Auth tokens and other sensitive items are stored in secure device storage where available.
  • We apply organizational and technical measures for the limited data we control (e.g., Supabase project security).

8) Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, rectify, delete, restrict, object, port your data, and withdraw consent (where applicable). For account data (e.g., email in Supabase), please contact us. For local notes, use the App’s features (e.g., delete/clear). Note: we cannot decrypt your notes and do not have access to them.


9) Children

The App is not intended for individuals under 13 (or another age set by local law). We do not knowingly collect data from such users.


10) Contact us

  • Developer: VeilKeep

11) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The new version takes effect upon publication in the App and/or on the website. See the “Last updated” date above.


Account deletion (Supabase)

You can request deletion of your account (email and related records in Supabase). Email us from the address linked to your account with the subject “Delete my VeilKeep account”. After deletion, sign‑in will no longer be possible. Your local notes remain on your device until you delete them.


Device permissions

  • Camera/Photos/Files — to add attachments to notes (data remains local).
  • Biometrics (Face ID/Touch ID) — for quick, secure access to the local vault (optional).

You can revoke permissions in device settings; some features may stop working.
